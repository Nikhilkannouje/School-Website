Our Mission

A DAV school agenda typically refers to the schedule or plan for the day, week, or term used in schools affiliated with the Dayanand Anglo-Vedic (DAV) educational institutions. These schools follow the values and teachings inspired by Swami Dayanand Saraswati, focusing on a blend of modern education with Vedic principles.

What Makes Us Unique

DAV schools emphasize Vedic teachings derived from the Arya Samaj movement founded by Swami Dayanand Saraswati. The goal is to foster character building, moral integrity, and spirituality alongside academic learning. Daily activities like morning prayers, meditation, and yoga are a part of the curriculum, instilling self-discipline, calmness, and mindfulness.
